About Me

My name is Jaime García, I'm 23 years old and I consider myself a deeply curious person. I've always had a constant need to understand things beyond the surface level, which is why I research everything that catches my attention. I don't like leaving questions unanswered; on the contrary, I enjoy the process of seeking answers, questioning, and learning on my own. I'm characterized by a restless and analytical mindset. I'm not only interested in acquiring information, but in understanding it, processing it, and making sense of it. This has led me to develop a self-taught approach, where constant learning is part of my daily life.
